About Us

The Celtic Knot Conference Kwara
local meetup 2023 aims to bring Wikimedians
in Kwara State and beyond together to learn,
edit and share their experiences of working on
minority and marginalized languages on the Wikimedia projects.
Our aim is to help them learn how to direct the flow of information
across languages to eliminate/reduce barriers and
create visibility for indigenous knowledge online.
